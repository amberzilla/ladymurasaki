LADY MURASAKI

Lady Murasaki, formed in July 2011, is a female-lead group from Salt Lake City. Our culturally diverse group makes for a different sound that is a fusion of rock, jazz, blues, lounge and pop with a dash of indie. We perform regularly at SLC's popular live venues and have released a self-titled EP in 2012 and 'Summer Fling' in June 2013. Our most notable performances include the Utah Arts Fest, Utah Pride Fest, Channel 2 Morning News, City Weekly Music Awards, SLUG Localized, Craft Lake City and Park City TV.
